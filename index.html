01

Overview

Sales Call Prep ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension built and operated by Enterprise Sales Engine (Louis Nonis, Melbourne, Australia). This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

The short version We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any server we operate. The Extension runs entirely within your browser. Your data stays with you.

By installing and using the Extension, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

02

What data we collect

The Extension stores the following data locally in your browser only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Your Anthropic API key — stored locally, used only to make calls to the Anthropic API on your behalf
  • Your name and company — entered optionally in Settings, used to personalise brief output
  • Your brief usage count — tracks how many briefs you have generated (for the free trial limit)
  • Your licence key and email — stored locally if you upgrade to Pro

None of this data is ever transmitted to Enterprise Sales Engine servers. It lives only in your Chrome browser profile.

03

What we do NOT collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your browsing history or activity
  • The LinkedIn profiles you view or generate briefs for
  • The content of briefs generated
  • Your IP address or location
  • Any analytics, telemetry, or usage data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Any information from other tabs or websites
04

Local storage

All Extension data is stored using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. This data is:

  • Stored only on your device, in your Chrome profile
  • Never synced to Chrome Sync or any cloud service by the Extension
  • Cleared automatically if you uninstall the Extension
  • Fully within your control — you can clear it at any time via Chrome's extension settings
05

Third-party services

The Extension interacts with two third-party services:

Anthropic API When you generate a brief, the Extension sends the LinkedIn URL and any context you provide directly to the Anthropic API using your own API key. This data is processed by Anthropic subject to their Privacy Policy. Enterprise Sales Engine does not receive or store this data.
Stripe If you upgrade to Pro, payment is processed by Stripe. Stripe collects your payment and email details subject to their Privacy Policy. Enterprise Sales Engine receives only confirmation that a payment was made.
06

LinkedIn data

When you generate a brief, the Extension reads the visible text content of the LinkedIn profile page you are currently viewing. This data is:

  • Read directly from the page in your browser — not accessed via LinkedIn's API
  • Sent directly to the Anthropic API using your own key
  • Never stored, logged, or transmitted to Enterprise Sales Engine
  • Not retained after the brief is generated

Use of this Extension is subject to LinkedIn's Terms of Service. You are responsible for ensuring your use complies with LinkedIn's terms.

07

Children's privacy

The Extension is designed for professional use by adults. It is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

08
